About Us

Who we are

Out of the Box (OOTB) is a fun and exciting, all-in-one smart solution designed to help you to have a better advance movie screening experience.

Reserve screening tickets, load some coupons, check lining status, and enjoy the movie. OOTB is the one of a kind convenient survival kit for movie lovers.

How it Works


1. Find the movie you want to watch.

2. RSVP via our website or mobile app.

3. Save RSVP info to OOTB card, you may optionally save our recommended food vouchers and/or coupons to nearby restaurants.

4. Tap your OOTB card on the kiosks located in theater to check-in and activate your coupons. You will also check seating status in realtime from the kiosk or our mobile app.
